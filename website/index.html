Everything You Need on the Court

From scheduling to stats, PickleGo keeps your pickleball life organized.

Match Organization

Set up singles or doubles with customizable settings — game count, point targets, date, time, and location.

Open Match Invites

Share a link and let anyone join. When spots fill, a waitlist keeps players queued — they auto-promote when a spot opens.

Performance Tracking

Wins, losses, win rates, streaks, head-to-head rivalries, and partner-specific doubles stats — all tracked automatically.

Live Scoring

Score rally by rally on your iPhone or Apple Watch. Track points-to-win and best-of series with real-time sync.

Player Network

Connect with players via text, email, or shareable links. Build your crew and track partnership stats.

Team Randomizer

Shuffle teams between games for fair and fun matchups. Keep things fresh with random assignments.

Notifications

Stay in the loop with customizable notifications for match invites, updates, and more.

Courts Discovery

Find pickleball courts near you on an interactive map. Save your favorites for quick access.

Score From Your Wrist

The PickleGo Apple Watch app lets you score rallies without touching your phone.

  • Tap to score each rally right from your wrist
  • See live game scores and series progress
  • Syncs automatically with your iPhone
  • Works for singles and doubles
