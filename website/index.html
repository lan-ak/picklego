Everything You Need on the Court

From scheduling to stats, PickleGo keeps your pickleball life organized.

Match Organization

Set up singles or doubles with customizable settings — game count, point targets, date, time, and location.

Open Match Invites

Share a link and let anyone join your match. Teams auto-assign when full, and everyone gets notified.

Performance Tracking

Wins, losses, win rates, streaks, head-to-head records, and partner-specific doubles stats — all in one place.

Game Scoring

Track scores game by game with configurable points-to-win and best-of series support.

Player Network

Connect with players via text, email, or shareable links. Build your crew and track partnership stats.

Team Randomizer

Shuffle teams between games for fair and fun matchups. Keep things fresh with random assignments.

Notifications

Stay in the loop with customizable notifications for match invites, updates, and more.

Courts Discovery

Find pickleball courts near you on an interactive map. Save your favorites for quick access.
